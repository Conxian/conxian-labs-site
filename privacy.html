Privacy Protocol

System Date: February 16, 2026

1. Zero-Knowledge Philosophy

Conxian-Labs (Pty) Ltd operates on a non-custodial basis. We do not collect, store, or transmit your private keys, seed phrases, or individual transaction histories. Your sovereignty is maintained by ensuring all cryptographic operations occur locally on your device.

2. Public Infrastructure

Our applications interact with public decentralized networks (Bitcoin, Stacks). Any transaction data broadcast to these networks is public by design and inherent to the operation of the protocol.

3. Data Collection

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that de-anonymize our users. Any communication with our support or admin emails is handled with strict confidentiality and used solely for the intended purpose.

4. Contact

For inquiries regarding our privacy standards: admin@conxian-labs.com
