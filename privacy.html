Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. No Personal Data Collection

Conxian-Labs (Pty) Ltd builds non-custodial software ("Conxius Wallet"). We do not collect, store, or transmit your private keys, seed phrases, or transaction history. All cryptographic operations occur locally on your device.

Our applications interact with public blockchains (Bitcoin, Stacks). Transaction data broadcast to these networks is public by design.
